Registration by Check or Money Order

If you cannot pay by credit card, or are an organization like a school or corporation that requires all payments to go through purchase orders, do not worry!

To pay by check or money order, follow these instructions:

  1. Fill out a purchase order form (you can use our template or make your own) with the number of tickets and the names of the attendees.
  2. Mail the form to the following address: 
                            STEVENS ANIME CLUB
                        1 CASTLE POINT TER
                        HOWE BLDG FL 10
                        HOBOKEN NJ  07030
  3. We will reply with your tickets and an invoice.
  4. Within thirty (30) days, mail us a check or money order made out to "Stevens Anime Club" for the amount on the invoice. Put your invoice number in the check / money order memo.
