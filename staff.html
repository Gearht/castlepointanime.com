The Castle Point Anime Convention is organized and operated by the Stevens Anime Club, a student organization under the Student Government Association at the Stevens Institute of Technology.

We are all students from con chair all the way down to our gophers and volunteers (well, with the exception of some alumni volunteers). CPAC is not only an event for our attendees to have fun at, but also a learning and service opportunity for all staff involved. Because of this we try and make our convention as official and complete as possible, that way attendees get the best experience and our staff get real-world experience in event management.

Below are some profiles of our staff. (If you are in high school and want to be on this page, come to Stevens!)
